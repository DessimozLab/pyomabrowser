Suggesting a new or updated genome for OMA

Are you missing an important genome in OMA? Is our coverage in a certain region of the phylogenetic tree underrepresented? Or did we miss out on an crucial update of your genome of interest? Please let us know with the form below and we will try to do our best to include your suggestion in the next possible release.

The genomes requested must have a publicly available protein annotation in order to be included in OMA.

Keep in mind that computational efforts are quite demanding and we might not be able to include your suggestion straight away. We prioritise the suggestions based on genome quality, importance, and easiness to include. We therefore recommend to complete the form to the best of your knowledge and to be available by email for further clarifications if needed later on.

OMA Visiting Fellowship

The OMA Visiting Fellowship program aims at facilitating collaborative visits between researchers working on a project that could benefit from interaction or collaboration with the OMA team, or interested in initiating such project.

Visit length is flexible and could vary from a few weeks to a several months. The fellowship covers travel and accommodation expenses during the duration of the visit.

The OMA Visiting Fellowship programme would be particularly suited to PhD students or postdocs with direct experience using OMA, but this is not a requirement. Likewise, applicants will be typically on leave from their institution, but independent researchers are also eligible.

To apply, send a brief CV and a statement of interest briefly outlining the collaborative project foreseen (~1 page), the duration of the visit, and a potential start date. The submission form is available at http://omabrowser.org/fellowship

For informal queries, contact Dr. Adrian Altenhoff, OMA project manager at adrian.altenhoff@inf.ethz.ch.

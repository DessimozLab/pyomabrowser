Predicting Gene Ontology function to your sequences

Are you interested in annotating gene ontology functions to your own proteome? Here, we provide an easy way to project functions from the entire OMA Browser based on sequence similarity to your sequences.

Please upload a fasta formatted file of maximum {{max_upload_size | floatformat }}MB with your sequences (might also be in gzip or bzip compressed format). The service identifies the closest sequence in a very quick manner and assigns the known GO annotations to it. We return a gaf2.1 formatted file to you that contain all the annotations and as well the source protein from which the function has been projected from.

Typical uses


OMA browser is a web-interface-database for orthology inference, many uses are possibles according to queries and the inference users need. We have summup the typicals uses you can do with oma browser and what kind of result you will get.


What would you like to do ?

  • Propagate functional annotations between two genomes
  • Identify all orthologs of a gene in a given set of organisms
  • Allign two whole genomes
  • Align protein–protein interaction networks between two genomes
  • Identify all genes in vertebrates that descended from the ancestral β-hemoglobin
  • Identify orthologs to infer a species tree

Appropriate orthology type

OMA groups with high-coverage of the species of interest

Comments

Since, barring classification error, all sequence pairs within an OMA group are orthologs, the corresponding gene tree is expected to follow the species tree.
